About

Founded in 1992, Style Textile is a well-known textile and apparell manuifacturer operating out of Lahore Pakistan. Style is engaged in innovation, manufacturing and providing end-to-end apparell solutions to partner such as Adidas, Hurley, Levis, Nike, Reebok and Tom Tailor amongst other reowned global brands.

Style employs a large multinational work force in its facilities that are located around Lahor. The fully vertically integrated facilities help give the organization the infrastructure, knowledge, logistics and speed to meet the shortest possible lead times that are need of present day customer. Style's reputation for ethical and sustainable business practices is acknowledged and respected by its customers, employees and the community at large.

Capabilities

Equipped with state of the art technology, world class knowledge and over two decades of experience, style produces the first knitted fabrics. We have the capability of manufacturing various varieties of knitted fabrics such as single jersey, pique, ribs, interlocks, thermals, fleece and plaited fabrics amongst others. These fabrics in cotton, blends, and synthetics can then be dyed and finished with the latest performance features like water repellant, low shrinkage, silk wash, moisture management etc.

To consolidate our position as an industry leader, we are committed to the continuous improvement and development of our people, products, technology and processes.

Research and Development

In house laboratories

Sustainability

As a leader in Style Textile and apparel industry, we understand and appreciate the responsibilty we carry towards the environment and to our community. Being compliant is an integral part of Style's culture and this is demonstrated in our commitment to conduct business in a reesponsible and ethical manner.

Style has dedicated team of experts in the CSR department who report directly to our CEO. This team is responsible for training and advising our management and staff on all matters related to CSR. In addition, they also communicate with customers about labor, environment, health and safety matters at Style. Since this is continuous process, our team work closely with our buyers, local and international organizations to discuss ways of bringing improvements to our workplace. This commitment to global standards of compliance is duly reflected through our certifications of ISO 14001, WRAP and OHSAS 18001.
